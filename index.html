About Us

With the ever-rising hustle of life, people are finding it extremely difficult to go about their daily life chores while also maintaining dynamic careers. Time is money; this maxim has gained more importance than ever today when people and money always seem to be on the go. But the chores of daily life cannot wait and to this end, we provide you with a service that cuts one such hassler from your life.

Our L.M.A.D.S. service will pick up clothes from your house, and deliver them back after thorough washing/dry-cleaning and ironing. This would not only save our clients time but also money from buying and maintaining expensive cleaning apparatus.

Washing machines no doubt provide a lot of efficiency in cleaning clothes at home, but the problem of time still dawns on the daily hustlers who are building the lives of their dreams. We take care of that for you! We will wash your clothes for you while you chase your dreams. Next time you ace that goal you are working towards, thank L.M.A.D.S. for taking care of the stuff that’s trivial for achievers like you!

Administration

Admin

Our laundry team and administration is very well versed in handling our client's clothes and take care of their belongings.

Washing Stage

Laundry attendant

The picked up clothes are first taken to our nearest laundry centre & washed thoroughly which cleans & sanitizes the cloth completely and ensures that the clothes are delivered to the customer completely new.

Drying Stage

Drying Clerk

In , the next step the cloth is completely dried using solar radiation which ensures that no further germs can thrive in the cloth after washing & no traces of any water are left in the cloth fabric after the washing procedure.

Ironing Stage

Ironing Officer

The next step involves removing the creases and folds in the cloth fabric & ironing all the wrinkles out so that the cloth becomes more presentable & so that the cloth becomes ready to be shipped back.

Packaging Stage

Packing Officer

The last & the final step involves packaging the clients clothes in an organized manner so that when delivered the clothes reach the desired party safely and mannerly.
